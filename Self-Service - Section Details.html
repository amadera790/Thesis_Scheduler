AE300/Lecture/1 - The Uses of Theory in Art Education | Credits 4.00
This course is an examination of the philosophy, psychology and literature of art education as the basis for developing and evaluating art curriculum and instruction. It offers appropriate field experience to apply theory to practical problems of teaching. It provides opportunity to examine and design instructional material and methods. Offered in even years. Prerequisites: ED 210, ED 212; or permission of the instructor.
